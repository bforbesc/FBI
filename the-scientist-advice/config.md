## Language & Tone

- Plain language — no jargon without a one-sentence explanation
- Spell out acronyms the first time
- Translate benchmarks into practical meaning, not raw numbers
- Use analogies when a concept is abstract
- Audience is technical but not academic — they need to act on findings, not just read them
